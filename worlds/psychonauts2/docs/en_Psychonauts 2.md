# Psychonauts 2

## Where is the settings page?

The [player settings page for this game](../player-settings) contains all the options
for configuring your randomiser experience.

## What does randomisation do to this game?

All collectable items in Psychonauts 2 are shuffled into the multiworld item pool.
This includes:

- **Psi Cards** – Hidden cards in hub areas and mental worlds.
- **Supply Chests & Keys** – Locked chests and the keys that open them.
- **Psy Challenge Markers** – Markers that unlock psychic challenges.
- **Scavenger-Hunt Items** – 16 unique collectibles scattered across hub areas.
- **Outfits** – Costume pieces unlocked by completing the scavenger hunt.
- **Figment Milestones** – Rewards at 20 %, 40 %, 60 %, 80 %, and 100 % figment
  completion in each mental world.
- **Memory Vaults, Nuggets of Wisdom, Half-a-Minds** – Mental-world collectibles.
- **Dufflebags, Steamer Trunks, Hatboxes, Suitcases** – Luggage items in mental worlds.
- **Shop Items** – Items purchased from Otto's shop, including Otto-Shot filters,
  Lev Ball skins, Projection upgrades, and consumables.
- **Rank-Up Rewards** – Items received for each Psychonaut rank point earned.
- **Psychic-Ability Unlocks** – Obtaining these checks teaches a psychic ability or
  upgrade (as a progressive item).
- **Mental-World Story Completions** – Completing a mental world grants an item that
  may unlock post-completion content in that world.
- **Area-Access Passes** – Keys to each hub area and mental world.

## What is the goal when randomised?

Your goal is to **defeat Maligula** by interacting with the Astralathe in Green Needle
Gulch.  The exact requirements depend on your chosen win condition:

| Win Condition | Requirements |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Normal** | Core abilities + key area accesses |
| **All Bosses** | All 8 abilities + 4 specific mental-world completions + Maligula Access |
| **All Scav Hunt** | All 16 scavenger-hunt items + Maligula Access |
| **Scav Hunt and Maligula** | All 16 scavenger-hunt items |

## How do progressive items work?

**Psychic abilities** (Telekinesis, Psi Blast, Pyrokinesis, Levitation, Clairvoyance,
Mental Connection, Time Bubble, Projection) each exist in the pool as four progressive
copies.  Finding the first copy grants the base ability; each additional copy grants
the next upgrade tier.

**Inventory upgrades** work the same way:
- Mind's Eyelets → Expandolier (2 copies of *Progressive Carry Capacity*)
- Fluff Pockets → Jumbo Fluff Pouch (2 copies of *Progressive Fluff Pouch*)
- Psifold Wallet → Astral Wallet (2 copies of *Progressive Wallet*)

## What does another world's item look like in Psychonauts 2?

Items belonging to other players are displayed as generic "Archipelago Item" pickups in
the game world.  Psychonauts 2's item-display UI will show the item's game and player
name once received.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?

When an item is received from another player, the Psychonauts 2 AP mod immediately
grants that item to Raz.  Ability items raise the appropriate ability tier, access
items unlock the corresponding area or mental world, and other items are added to the
appropriate inventory category.
